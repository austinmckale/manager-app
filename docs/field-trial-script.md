# Field Trial Script (Crew)

## 1) Start of Day
- Open `/today`.
- Tap your scheduled job.
- Start timer before work begins.

## 2) During Work
- Add progress photos (stage + area).
- Add receipts the moment materials are purchased.
- Keep timer running or stop/start around breaks.

## 3) End of Day
- Stop timer.
- Confirm all receipts/photos are uploaded (or queued if offline).
- Open job hub and update punch-list items.

## 4) Job Closeout (Owner/Admin)
- Ensure at least one `AFTER` photo exists.
- Ensure no open punch-list tasks remain.
- Ensure at least one non-draft invoice exists.
- Move job status to `COMPLETED`.

## 5) Weekly Review
- Check `/dashboard` KPIs.
- Export `/reports` CSVs for time, expenses, profitability.
